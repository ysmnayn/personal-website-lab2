More About Me ꩜ .ᐟ

Believe it or not, I have never planned on becoming a programmer. As a child, I dreamed of being a dentist, but life had other plans for me.

When I started university, I was introduced to coding for the very first time. It was both challenging and exciting, but most of all, it was rewarding. I worked hard to catch up, and before I knew it, I fell in love with programming!

Today, I enjoy problem-solving, designing user-friendly interfaces, and exploring the endless possibilities that software engineering offers. Looking back, I wouldn’t trade this journey for anything!
